An attempt to avoid the subjectivity of purpose...

This purpose of this entity is to serve as an ideological monument to the ethically irrelevant research performed in the pursuit of  arguably elegant, yet inarguably beautiful, crime...

The exploits of these pursuits represent themselves by what the artificer of this particular entity judges to be not the result of the daring, but the vast negative space of ideas brought to fruition by the vastly mobilizing fear experienced by the generality of the human population during the manifestation of what the majority of English speakers debatably refer to as "rational thought".

Therefore it is simply out of the nihilistic yet poignantly hopeful mind of an intrepid explorer into all things subversive that the diatribe encapsulated within this physically intangible vessel seeks to obtain pronunciation in the shared greatest common factor of the lowest common denominator of  human concepts concepts intermittently referred to as "culture". As such, its proprietor chooses to leave you with a hopefully in-dubious axiom for which to base upon what the reader assumedly identifies as their "take away" from this is...

Most people would rather die than think and many of them do!

Bertrand Russell
